Opportunities

I am looking for a self-motivated student who is interested in pursuing a Ph.D. degree in cybersecurity at Purdue University – West Lafayette, USA, starting in Spring or Fall 2019. Full financial support will be provided to students admitted into the Ph.D. program.

Prospective students should choose to enroll in the Department of Computer Information and Technology. Students who are imaginative and intend to think big in their work and life, and also meet the following requirements are encouraged to apply:

  • B.S. or M.S. degree in CS, EE or a relevant area (Students with B.S. degrees should apply for our direct-Ph.D. program.)

  • Excellent academic record

  • Excellent communication and writing skills

  • Some prior research experiences in the Internet of Things, AI, wireless networks, embedded systems, mobile computing, programming language, and operating system, are preferable

  • Background in computer/network security or cryptography is a plus, but not necessary

Please send your CV, academic transcripts, GRE/TOEFL scores, and other supporting documents as ONE PDF attachment to me.

Visiting/exchange scholars and students who have matching interests and strong research backgrounds are also welcome to contact me.
