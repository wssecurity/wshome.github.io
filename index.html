About me

I hold two Ph.D. degrees, one in computer science from complex network and cybersecurity research lab (CNSR) at Virginia Tech in 2018 and the other in cryptography from Xidian University in 2014. My research covers various topics in cybersecurity with the focus on security and privacy in networked information systems and cyber-physical systems, including cloud computing, Internet of Things, blockchains, computer/mobile systems, and wireless network.
